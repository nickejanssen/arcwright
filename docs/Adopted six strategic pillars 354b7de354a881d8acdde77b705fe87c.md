# Adopted six strategic pillars

Date: May 1, 2026
Rationale: Pillars: (1) Earned-evolution; (2) Founder-controlled and profit-first; (3) Platform and games both get protected time; (4) Human-led, AI-augmented; (5) Quality and ambition over scale and speed; (6) Build for unrepeatability. Original draft had seven candidates; pillars 4 and 5 (human-led + co-authorship) collapsed into one. Renamed 'Pixar-first, Valve-second, Stripe-later' to 'earned-evolution' for less informal phrasing.
