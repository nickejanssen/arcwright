# Nightcap v1 success criteria: qualitative bar is the gate, quantitative metrics are the instrument

Date: May 5, 2026
Rationale: Qualitative gate: five or more completed sessions with real groups outside the founder's immediate circle expressing unprompted enthusiasm. Quantitative instruments: session completion rate, arc pacing variance, killer concealment rate, replay intent. Metrics diagnose what to iterate on; they do not define shipped.
