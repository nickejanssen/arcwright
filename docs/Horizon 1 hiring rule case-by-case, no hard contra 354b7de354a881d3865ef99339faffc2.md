# Horizon 1 hiring rule: case-by-case, no hard contractor-only mandate

Date: May 1, 2026
Rationale: Default leans toward contractors. Equity-bearing collaborators are an exception requiring deliberate written justification, vesting schedule (4-year with 1-year cliff standard), and reverse-vesting clause. Looser than original 'contractors only' rule, but with guardrails to prevent casual equity grants in early stage.
