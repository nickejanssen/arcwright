# Balance principle is an engine-enforced constraint: clue chain always sufficient for full investigator engagement, interference throttled against balance state

Date: May 15, 2026
Rationale: The session must remain genuinely winnable for both sides at all times. Genuine clue chain has enough redundancy that no single interference event makes the case unsolvable. Killer interference opportunities are throttled against the session balance state. False clues are always falsifiable given the complete genuine evidence set. This principle governs clue distribution, puzzle gating, and killer action opportunity surfacing.
