# North star progression confirmed: Pixar to Valve to Stripe (B to A)

Date: May 5, 2026
Rationale: Arcwright starts as a creative studio with internal infrastructure (Pixar-shape), grows to studio-plus-platform with a small external developer base (Valve-shape), and matures into a platform-first company with an active studio (Stripe-shape). Games are the primary product and identity through at least H1 and H2. The platform API opens to external developers only after internal games have proven it. This progression was implicit in the strategy document; this session made it explicit as a filter for all product decisions.
