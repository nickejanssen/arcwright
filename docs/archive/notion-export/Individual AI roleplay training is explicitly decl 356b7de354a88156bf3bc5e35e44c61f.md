# Individual AI roleplay training is explicitly declined as an Arcwright market category

Date: May 4, 2026
Rationale: This category (sales roleplay, skills training tools) is crowded and requires deep LMS/CRM integration that is incompatible with Arcwright's current stage and platform direction. Adaptive individual learning (e.g., DuoLingo-style personalization) is a separate, future vertical signal that may be on-mission post-MVP but is not the same category and is not a current priority.
Section: Section 4: Vertical Strategy
Status: Committed
