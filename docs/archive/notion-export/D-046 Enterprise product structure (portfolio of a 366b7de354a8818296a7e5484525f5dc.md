# D-046 Enterprise product structure (portfolio of arcs on same platform)

Date: May 19, 2026
Rationale: Arcwright's enterprise team-building offering is a portfolio of arc templates running on the same Arcwright platform that powers Nightcap consumer. Arcs for enterprise are purpose-built (leadership development, team formation, onboarding narratives) or adapted from Nightcap. The platform is the same; the products are distinct. Resolves the open question about enterprise as distinct arc type vs same product with different rails.
