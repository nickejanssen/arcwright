# Platform name committed: Arcwright

Date: May 3, 2026
Rationale: Arcwright selected as platform name after a two-round candidate process. Compound coinword: arc (narrative arc, structural span, precision) plus wright (craftsperson, as in playwright, wheelwright). Cleared all eight due-diligence checks: no trademark conflicts found, http://arcwright.com/.ai/.io appear unregistered, social handles available, no negative linguistic associations, no active brand conflicts in adjacent spaces. Pretentiousness flag (wright suffix not universally known) raised and consciously accepted. Finalists considered before Arcwright was selected: Narrata (eliminated: http://narrat.dev active in same space), Lore (eliminated: generic in gaming, not trademarkable), PlotForge (eliminated: three active competitors using exact name). See 04-Naming-and-Brand-v1 for full due diligence record.
