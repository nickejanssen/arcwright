# The Arcwright platform moat is personalization depth, not authoring tooling

Date: May 4, 2026
Rationale: Authoring tooling can be commoditized. The depth of runtime personalization (how well the platform understands who is in the room, their history, preferences, and relationships) is the differentiator that determines the ceiling of what any designed arc can produce. This is an architectural priority, not a product features priority.
