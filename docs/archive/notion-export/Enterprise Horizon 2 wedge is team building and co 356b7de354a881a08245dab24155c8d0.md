# Enterprise Horizon 2 wedge is team building and corporate events (combined), explicitly not individual corporate training

Date: May 4, 2026
Rationale: Competitive analysis confirmed the individual AI roleplay training market (Second Nature, Mindtickle, Hyperbound, etc.) is crowded, commoditizing, and requires deep LMS/CRM integration from day one. It is the wrong wedge. The enterprise category of AI-powered group narrative experience for team building does not yet exist as a recognized product category. No established competitor is selling it. This is the open territory for Horizon 2.
