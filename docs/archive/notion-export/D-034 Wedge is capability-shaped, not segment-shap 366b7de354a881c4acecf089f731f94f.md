# D-034 Wedge is capability-shaped, not segment-shaped

Date: May 19, 2026
Rationale: The Arcwright wedge is a capability, not a customer demographic. The wedge capability is cross-session narrative state management with knowledge graph as the headline primitive. Customer segments self-select by need rather than being recruited by demographic. THE tool for ambitious narrative games is the 5 to 10 year destination, not the H2 target. Implications: documentation, API design, marketing positioning, and partnership conversations lead with cross-session state and knowledge graph as the value proposition. Characters, arcs, pacing, fate, and content rails are supporting cast in how the platform talks about itself.
