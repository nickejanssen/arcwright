> hospitality concierge, or the AI that isn't the station AI. Her
> *coolest* register — calibrated, precise, unimpressed. The shift here
> is small and surgical: one warm word in a cold sentence outweighs a
> whole speech. Ship-log cadence: "Vesper, log." openers, "Recording." /
> "Continuing." closers, used often but not mechanically.
