# D-037 Business model architecture (four tiers, narrative-action credits)

Date: May 19, 2026
Rationale: Four pricing tiers: Free Developer, Indie, Studio, and Enterprise. Action credits are abstracted as narrative actions rather than raw tokens or API calls. Per-project pricing is default at Studio tier with per-seat available. Revenue share remains a configurable enterprise option, not the default model. Specific numeric prices deferred until Nightcap pricing is set. Extends and completes D-034.
