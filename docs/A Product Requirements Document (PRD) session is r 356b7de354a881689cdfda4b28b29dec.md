# A Product Requirements Document (PRD) session is required before Chat 6 (Technical Architecture)

Date: May 4, 2026
Rationale: Without a PRD, Chat 6 becomes half requirements-gathering and half architecture, which wastes the architecture session. A lightweight PRD (1-2 pages capturing what the Arcwright engine must do, key design constraints, non-negotiables, integration surfaces) gives Chat 6 clear requirements to design against.
