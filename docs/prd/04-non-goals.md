# Non-Goals

## What the Platform Must Not Do

- Must not assume a specific delivery surface at the engine layer.
- Must not treat AI as the creative author.
- Must not allow knowledge state management to be skipped or made optional.
- Must not expose model routing decisions or model names to end players.
- Must not hard-code a dependency on any specific AI provider, model, or model version.
- Must not be designed in a way that requires a rebuild to support emergent narrative arcs (monster RPG) or multi-team enterprise sessions.
- Must not launch without content safety architecture active.
- Must not assume a specific team size or build capacity in its requirements.
- Must not include authoring capabilities that only Nightcap needs and classify them as platform capabilities.
- Must not ship without session telemetry instrumented.

## Open Questions and Deferred Decisions

**Critical:** Nightcap trademark clearance (counsel not yet engaged; known conflicts: Studio Nightcap and NBA Nightcap). Domain and social handle registration (urgent action item).

**High:** What does the enterprise team-building product look like as a distinct arc? When does the platform open to external developers and what is the selection process? Which role does the first co-founder fill? Third game selection against platform capability gaps. Open source self-hosting break-even calculation (Chat 6 deliverable). External developer demand validation: before the H2 closed developer beta opens, show the internal API documentation and arc schema format to at least 10 target external developers and record their feedback and pilot intent; the beta should not open on the basis of assumed demand. Enterprise buyer receptiveness: as soon as a working consumer prototype or demo footage exists, conduct brief interviews with 10 enterprise buyers across HR, L&D, and events manager roles using the demo footage; this should happen in H1, not at H2 entry.

**Medium:** Monster RPG arc design (Chat 8). H2-to-H3 transition signal thresholds. Mission-aligned pricing tier structure (H3+). Full telemetry schema design (Chat 6 deliverable).

**Parked:** Adaptive individual learning as future vertical. No-code arc builder for non-developer creators.
