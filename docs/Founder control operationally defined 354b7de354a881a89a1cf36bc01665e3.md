# Founder control operationally defined

Date: May 1, 2026
Rationale: Founder-controlled means: 51%+ voting control indefinitely, 51%+ economic ownership through capital-efficient growth as long as feasible, no unilateral investor power to redirect mission/force exit/remove founder/override strategy without founder consent, board seats earned by contribution not granted as permanent authority. Provides operational definition that capital strategy can reference when evaluating term sheets.
