# D-032 Brand approach is soft bifurcation

Date: May 19, 2026
Rationale: One legal entity. Two product brands inside it. Arcwright (or Arcwright Engine, final platform brand name deferred to dedicated naming chat) is the platform-facing identity for developers and enterprise. Arcwright Studios is the consumer-facing publishing label for games. Platform-facing web properties, documentation, enterprise contracts, and developer materials lead with the platform brand. Studio brand appears on game splash screens, App Store and Steam pages, consumer site, and press for game releases. Two trademark filings inside one corporate entity. Decision absorbs the audit's brand-neutrality concern while preserving operational simplicity for a solo founder.
